# PretextChat Launch Copy

## 1. Product Hunt Tagline

PretextChat is a multi-instance desktop client for people who use multiple AI tools and tasks every day.

## 2. Product Hunt Short Description

Stop managing AI in chaotic browser tabs. PretextChat turns ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, and more into organized task-oriented workspaces you can reopen, rename, and switch between.

## 3. Product Hunt First Comment

Hi Product Hunt,

I built PretextChat because my daily AI workflow had outgrown the browser.

I was constantly juggling ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity in messy tabs, often with multiple task contexts inside the same tool. Browser tabs are great for browsing, but not great for AI work.

PretextChat is my attempt to turn AI websites into task-oriented desktop workspaces:

- open multiple instances of the same AI product
- rename them by task
- reopen recent tasks quickly
- restore your last working session
- keep AI work separate from normal browsing

This version is still early, and I’m especially looking for feedback from people who use multiple AI tools every day.

The question I care about most is simple:

Would you rather manage your AI work here than in browser tabs?

## 5. Show HN Post

I built PretextChat because I realized my AI workflow no longer fit inside normal browser tabs.

I was using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity every day, often with multiple parallel tasks in each. The browser made all of that feel noisy and fragile:

- too many tabs
- poor task separation
- hard to reopen the right context
- awkward switching between long conversations

PretextChat is a desktop client focused only on AI workflows.

Current version supports:

- multiple instances per AI app
- task-style instance rename
- recent task reopen
- session restore
- login-state persistence

Longer term, I want to push it deeper into a real AI workbench, especially around text-heavy workflows. The project is also influenced by Pretext, a text layout engine I want to use for more stable AI UI interactions over time.

I’d love feedback from people who already use multiple AI tools every day.

The biggest thing I want to learn is whether this feels meaningfully better than staying in the browser.

## 7. Chinese Launch Post

如果你每天都在 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini、Perplexity 这些 AI 之间来回切换，浏览器标签页其实很快就不够用了。

我做了一个产品叫 PretextChat，想解决的不是“怎么接更多模型”，而是“怎么更好地管理 AI 工作流”。

它现在做的事情很简单：

- 把 AI 网站变成桌面客户端入口
- 同一个 AI 可以多开多个独立实例
- 每个实例都可以按任务重命名
- 可以快速重开最近任务
- 可以恢复上次工作状态

它更像是一个面向重度 AI 用户的工作客户端，而不是一个普通浏览器。

如果你本来就每天会同时开很多 AI，我特别想知道一件事：

你会不会愿意用它替代一部分浏览器里的 AI 标签页？

## 8. Short Demo Script

建议 20-30 秒演示按这个顺序录：

1. 首页显示多个 AI 应用
2. 连续打开 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini
3. 将 3 个实例分别命名为“写方案”“改代码”“查资料”
4. 在标签间快速切换
5. 关闭应用
6. 再次启动并恢复上次会话
7. 用一句收尾文案结束：`Stop managing AI in browser tabs.`
